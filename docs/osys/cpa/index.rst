This is a collection of some original manuscripts written in German by
|aosgdricst| with working state of 1985 til 1989. They were taken
without any changes but some formal corrections and enhancements.

The original manuscripts [#f1]_ partly in a historical WordStar 4 format
were multiple published on:

* November 27, 2002 by the German `Computer Museum, University of Stuttgart`_
  in their `FTP area for PC1715`_
* November 26, 2010 at the German web page by `Holm Tiffe`_ in his
  `CP/A Area (HT)`_
* February 17, 2012 by the German Working Group `Digital AG`_ in their
  download archive `Bürocomputer 8-Bit`_
* August 31, 2013 at the German web page by `Volker Pohlers`_ in his
  `CP/A Area (VP)`_.
